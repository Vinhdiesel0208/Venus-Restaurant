About

Venus

Welcome to Venus Restaurant, nestled in the heart of Saigon where we specialize in authentic Asian hotpot. Our venue offers a culinary journey through the flavors of Asia with a variety of rich broths and an array of fresh ingredients. Each pot is a simmering blend of spices and seasonings, inviting you to dive into the tradition of sharing and savoring. Situated by the scenic Saigon River, Venus Restaurant provides not just a meal, but an experience where every sip and bite brings comfort and joy.
